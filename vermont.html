Vermont

Freedom and Unity

Vermont is one of the smallest states of the US, behind a few others at a similar scale.

Vermont has a lot of forests and well-preserved nature as well as the Green Mountains that gave the state its surname. It is pretty appreciated by tourists from all over the world for its landscapes, nature and local specificities like traditionnaly elaborated maple syrup.

Vermont

Freedom and Unity

Vermont is one of the smallest states of the US, behind a few others at a similar scale.

Vermont has a lot of forests and well-preserved nature as well as the Green Mountains that gave the state its surname. It is pretty appreciated by tourists from all over the world for its landscapes, nature and local specificities like traditionnaly elaborated maple syrup.

It initially belonged to the French along with several surrounding territories, until the Seven Years' War at the end of which they were forced to hand it off to the United Kingdom.

A local figure from Vermont is senator Bernard "Bernie" Sanders, a leading figure of socialism and left-wing movements in today's US society. He took part in the last two Democratic primaries for the presidential election and ended just behind the final candidates (Hillary Clinton and Joe Biden) each time.

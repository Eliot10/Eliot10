Alaska

North to the Future

Alaska is one of the states with the lowest total income but also the top 1 highest per person thanks to its diversified economy based on fishing and abundant natural resources in fossil energies as well as tourism and military bases.

It is the largest state of the US – slightly larger than Iran, for reference – and most of its surface it made of lakes, wetlands with permafrost, forests and overall very prominent nature.

It initially belonged to the Russian but the land was too hard to maintain and costed them way too much money so they had to sell it to the US for approximately 120 million of 2021 Swiss francs.

15% of the Alaskan population are indigenous natives and they have a great influence in the state's politics. The state has changed its vote tendency over the years but it usually supports Republican candidates in presidential elections.
